Losing Chase is a 1996 American television film directed by Kevin Bacon in his directorial debut.[3] It stars Helen Mirren, Kyra Sedgwick and Beau Bridges. It debuted at the 1996 Sundance Film Festival.[4] The film was created for Showtime[5] where it appeared on August 18[2] though it was later given a limited theatrical release in December following its positive reception. The film is set on the island Martha's Vineyard. It was nominated for three awards at the 54th Golden Globe Awards.[6]
