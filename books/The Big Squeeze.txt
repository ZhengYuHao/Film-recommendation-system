The Big Squeeze is a compilation album by Snoop Dogg. It will be followed by a "Making of..." DVD called Niggaracci Presents: Cooked Up Crack. Snoop Dogg produces most of the songs. The first single is "Hat 2 Tha Bacc" by Westurn Union. The Big Squeeze debuted at #71 on the Billboard 200 Chart and #5 on the Rap Albums Chart.[4] Being a Koch release, it also peaked at the Top Independent Albums Chart on the 4th spot.[5] As a compilation, it scored the third position on the related chart.[6]
